Peanut Butter Frosting

Description

Taken from Suzanne Stull: See all recipes for original

If you like peanut butter, you'll love this peanut butter frosting â€” great on chocolate cakes, or for a really delicious double whammy, try it on a peanut butter cake. If you don't have cream, you may substitute milk. Makes enough to frost one 2-layer 9-inch cake or one 9x13-inch cake.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 4 cups of confectioners' sugar
  • 1/3 cup of cream

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, beat butter and peanut butter until light and fluffy.
  2. Slowly beat in 1/2 of the confectioner's sugar.
  3. Mix in 1/4 cup of the cream. Beat in remaining confectioners' sugar.
  4. If necessary, add a little more cream or milk until the frosting reaches a good spreading consistency.
